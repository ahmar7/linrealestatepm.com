“Our interests are aligned with our Owners to ensure a cash flowing, hands-off investment.” – James Wachob

 

All aspects of managing your new rental property are already in place. Once you are under contract on a property you
love, your relationship with the property managers will begin as we go to work for you:

● Marketing your rental to obtain a qualified applicant
● Vetting and placing quality tenants
● Collecting monthly rent, on time
● Responding to maintenance requests, in a timely manner
● Communicating with tenants, and rental owners
● Keep your property occupied and cash-flowing so you get to sit back and enjoy your monthly rent payments as they are deposited into your account.

 

Of course, this is real estate and issues will come up. When these things happen, we are the team to have on your side.

 

Foundation Property Management manages numerous properties across the Memphis Area, and we have an excellent track record in managing properties for local and out-of-state investors. Because of our volume, we offer better prices on everything from roofing to plumbing and we pass those savings along to our investors. We employ a full-service staff
overseeing your properties, including an in-house maintenance team managed by licensed technicians.

 

With two decades of real estate background in real estate development, homebuilding, and property management James
Wachob recognized the need for a property management company focused on being aligned with the rental owner. He
created Foundation Property Management with a single guiding principle: To manage our client’s assets as if they were
his own. Our commitment to providing safe and clean properties to the Memphis workforce is of the utmost importance.
Each property managed by Foundation Property Management must meet our high standards and we refuse to manage
properties that do not fit these basic standards of living.

 

 

From first-time investors to our seasoned veterans, our team acts as your boots on the ground. Our highly-trained, market
experts handle all aspects of property management tailored to your individual needs. But the real value comes from the
quality of communication and transparent operations you receive from Memphis’ Fastest Growing Property Manager.

 

What else should you consider as you select a property manager? We believe the financial strength of the company is an
important consideration. Below are a few items for your consideration in determining our ability to be there for you, both
during the renovation process and after you close on your investment property.
We do not live on profiting from repairs to your rental portfolio. As a home builder, turnkey provider, and real estate
investor James offers the services of Foundation Property Management to better serve his clients. As proof of this, we use
a sliding scale when repairs need to be made. James is committed to his saying “If my investors are not winning, I am not
profiting”

 

We form a partnership with our investors, who are not merely looking for a commodity provider, they are growth oriented,
and they share our core values. Above all, our rental property owner-driven principles have allowed us to work
closely with each individual to gain insight into the needs and wants of those who matter most: you. We’ve come a long
way, and while our client base continues to grow, we never forget the most important thing of all – the families that
choose us as their investment property manager. As you continue to grow your portfolio and live out those dreams, know
we’ll be here for you every step of the way.
